Hornet spread in Germany

Three interactive maps tracking the invasive Asian hornet (Vespa velutina) across Germany, using the native European hornet (Vespa crabro) as a control to cancel out citizen-science observer effort and population-density bias. Data: GBIF occurrence records, 2014–2025.

1Sightings timeline

Every V. velutina sighting, month by month. Each marker lights up red in its month of observation and fades to grey thereafter.

2Velutina / crabro ratio — by Landkreis

Per-district choropleth of the velutina-to-crabro ratio, animated by year. Districts with too little signal render grey.

3Velutina / crabro ratio — 3D hexbin

The same ratio as animated 3D hexagon height across a topographic map — columns rise where the invasive share is climbing.
Built with terramap. Source & regeneration steps in the repository README.
